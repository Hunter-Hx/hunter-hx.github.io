After Everything

At night, when the tie is hung,
And the T-shirts lie scattered,
Socks on the floor,
The kitchen still warm with dinner.
When the wigs are removed,
And the makeup is washed,
The shelf in disarray,
And the vest is on,
The TV is muted and the assignment is are done,
The kids half-asleep and the doors locked,
That's when 'love' happens-
Or whatever they call it.

In A Dream Today

In a dream today,
You wore no veil.
I could see your ears
And it was not pierced.
In a dream today,
You wore no glasses
I could see your naked eye
I could tell their color.
And it was the first time I got to believe
That man is not a machine
But can be ignited by the sparkles in your eyes.
In a dream today,
You wore no shoes.
I could see your small feet
They're that of the Egyptian goddess.
In a dream today.
You wore no gloves.
I could see your slimy fingers.
And I could tell they fell cold.
It was the first time I got to believe.
That man is not an energy bulb.
But can be turned on by the flicky touch of your cold hands
By Kweku Crystal

A Song For Two

My fingers bleed out anytime I try to play guitar.
Its strings are too thin to strum.
So the melody in my head never gets out.
Here I am with a bow tuning love from the heart with my new violin.
This is why I would not leave the Lute family.
They made me miss the person I used to be.
So tonight
I deserve the rhythm of my music in pure silence.

Tonight my bandaged fingers will strum
The agony that travels from the tongue of the wrong mind
Crush your heart, and journey through your veins and hold it venom.
If it leaves your thoughts paralyzed
Then I'll be your healer.
To unveil your true desire.
Thus, "Solitude is not the doom you yearn for."

Her warm touch hurts like the truth.
Her smile is as beautiful as the sunset.
It's scary when she's silent.
Like a nightmare, her absence keeps me awake.
So I'll send you a bird to sing a song for two.
And when you're back, I'll play for you to stay.
By Kweku Crystal
