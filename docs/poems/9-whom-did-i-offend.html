Whom Did I Offend?

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

Who have I wronged?
To suffer until the end,
With every hope bent
And no friend.

Who have I wronged?
To soak my pillow with tears,
Endless sadness and fears,
Borrowing only to hear
It's all in arrears.

Who have I wronged?
That I stress my brain,
Sleep in the rain,
In vain,
But hope to one day reign.

Who have I wronged?
That everyone pretends,
I am no use to them,
Expelled like trash to fend
For myself, a broken blend.
Who have I wronged?
Who have I really wronged?
