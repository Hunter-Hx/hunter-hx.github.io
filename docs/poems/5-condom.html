Codom

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

One or two packets,
For a friend - not just anyone, mind you.
"Hey, can I have a packet or two?"
He asked the store clerk as he rushed off to work.
Yes, work - no need to ask too many questions.
Just a minute for a friend,
"It's cold out and please hurry,
My friend is waiting for me on the couch."
I'll be discreet in case I take too long -
Which of course I shouldn't.
Hurry so she won't have to wait too long.
And keep the change,
Thanks to the rubber latex,
We can be safe, at least for a while.
