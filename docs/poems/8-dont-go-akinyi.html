Dont Go Akinyi

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

Hold my hand, Akinyi,
Don't let our young love story end
I told you about her
I told you she requested it,
Don't let a hug ruin it, Akinyi,

Don't go, Akinyi,
One moment with her
And my mind is on you,
You know our love story,
Short and sweet
Why can't you hold me, Akinyi

Don't cry, Akinyi
It's you
And you know it,
I've never lied to you
So why are you leaving?

Roses aren't always red,
Violets aren't always blue,
Don't leave and leave me feeling blue,
I didn't know it would hurt you,
Come and lie
On my chest,
As a bubble I blew.

Don't go, Akinyi,
It'll be a shame
If our love story ends so quickly,
Come hold me, Akinyi
And I'll let her go.
