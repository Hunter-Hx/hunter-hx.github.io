A Day In Life

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

It's yet another day,
To make moves with snakes,
Part ways with gods,
And talk gospel with the devil,
Listen to love songs with broken hearts,
Pretend we are okay and;
Sit around the table with backbiters
Pin blames on situations,
Create animosity with pure hearts,
And conceal the evil with righteousness
Talk to the evil ones,
And plan on how to slay our wrong doers
And them who are better than them(us)
Hate the benevolent
And love the malevolent
Leaving a gap for the adiaphorous
It's yet another day,
Just like any other day.
