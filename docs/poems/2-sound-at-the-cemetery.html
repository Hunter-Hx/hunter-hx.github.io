Sound At The Cemetery

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

Untold mysteries and stories abound,
In eerie silence, peace can be found.
The cemetery holds secrets untold,
Where the dead lie in eternal hold.

From graves, monsters may arise,
Ghostly in nature, a haunting surprise.
Desolate and strange, the cemetery exudes,
A certain kind of feeling, hard to elude.

Amidst the stillness, a cricket's sound,
Pierces the air, a rare occurrence found.
Only in the dusky hours, it may be heard,
A haunting melody, both eerie and blurred.
