If We Just Friends

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

If we are just friends,
Let's keep a clear boundary,
And not fall into temptation's sin,
Leaving me in despair, and you with doubt,
Let's enjoy each other's company,
And be content with what we're about.

If we are just friends,
No need to call me late at night,
And speak of things beyond friendship's light,
But if you need me, I'm here,
To lend a listening ear.

If we are just friends,
Don't hurt yourself with false hope,
But cherish our bond, don't let it elope,
For friendship is a precious treasure,
That can last a lifetime, without measure.
