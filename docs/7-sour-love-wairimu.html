Codom

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

Wairimu,
Like a typhoon,
You've shaken my world,
And built your own
Wall of silence,
Unbreakable by my pleas,
But who will apologize first?

Wairimu,
Like the still waters,
I never expected it to run so deep,
Just a chat with Akinyi,
And you exploded with anger,
I enjoy her company,
She's a colleague, she's a friend,
Not so much with you,
Should we not have other friends?

Wairimu,
Like the tamarind,
Your love tasted sour,
And like garlic,
Mine too, I guess, was bitter,
Two tigers can't live on the same mountain,

Let's move on,
Let's separate everything,
What do you say?
I can't keep running circles in my mind.
